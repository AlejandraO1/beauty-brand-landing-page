Our Story

Then I Met You was founded by Charlotte Cho. She believes that a skin care routine should be a sensorial ritual. After living in South Korea for about 5 years, Charlotte developed a deep connection with her culture, her skin as well as her jeong. By combining Korean heritage ingredients such as fermented rice, ginseng and persimmon, as well as innovative science, Then I Met You gives you products that aid in your skin care routine while also being clinically effective.

When you have a profound relationship with your skincare routine, Then I Met You believes that you will enjoy the action of applying products which makes you apply products more consistently and thus reaping all of the benefits.

"With Then I Met You, I am going even deeper in my quest — not only curating, but crafting truly transformative skin care products that will stay a part of your life for years to come."

-Charlotte Cho, Founder of Then I Met You

Sustainability

There is a focus on sustainability through Then I Met You. Packages are 100% recyclable and are printed with soy ink. Their manufacturers and formulators are certified in providing safe and ethical working conditions across the supply chain. The products have clean and natural formulations.

Jeong In Action

Jeong is a Korean cultural value in which one can have a deep, meaniningful connection with people, places and things. The practice of Jeong is embodied within Then I Met You as its purpose is to help create a deep connection between people and their skin care rituals.
